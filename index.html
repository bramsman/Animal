Tiger

Tiger is one of the big cats in animal world, rivaled only by the Lion.
It hunts its prey for survival. Despite it being a wild animal, it can be use as a pet animal when properly trained. I love Tigers because of their amazing features such as speed, patience, stamina, strength, ability to adapt very fast to a new environment, all these and many more features makes it a great cat.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris.
  • Average Length: 4 metres (13 feet).
  • Average Lifespan: 10-26 years
  • Habitat: Dry grassland and Rainforest.
  • Nickname: Apex predator (without a natural predator or enemy).
  • Weight: 160-230 kg

Tiger usually hunts by night and preys on a variety of animals, but it prefers fairly large prey such as deer (sambar, chital, and swamp deer) and wild pigs.
